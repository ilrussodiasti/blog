About Victor Oshimen

Victor Oshimen is a Nigerian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Napoli and the Nigerian national team.

He was born on December 29, 1998 in Lagos, Nigeria.

Victor started his professional football career with the Nigerian club, Ultimate Strikers Academy, before moving on to play for clubs like Lille, Wolfsburg and now Napoli.

His First Goal for Napoli

On April 3, 2022, he scored his first goal for Napoli in a Serie A match against Udinese. It was a great feeling to score for his new team and help they secure the win.

Training with the Super Eagles

Last week, he joined up with the Nigerian national team for training ahead of their upcoming World Cup. It's always an honor to represent his country and he's looking forward to the matches.
