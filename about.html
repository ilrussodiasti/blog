About

Victor Oshimen is a professional football player from Nigeria, born on December 29, 1998. He currently plays as a forward for Napoli and the Nigerian national team.

Oshimen started his professional career with Ultimate Strikers Academy in Nigeria, before moving to Europe to play for VfL Wolfsburg in Germany. He then played for Charleroi and Lille in the French league, before signing with Napoli in Italy in 2020.

On the international level, Oshimen made his debut for the Nigerian national team in 2017 and has since been an important player for the team. He was also part of the Nigerian team that won the bronze medal at the 2019 Africa Cup of Nations.
